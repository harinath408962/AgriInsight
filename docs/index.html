AgriInsight

Smart Decision Support for Farmers

AgriInsight is a decision-support system that transforms real-time weather and agricultural market data into clear, actionable advisories to help farmers make informed planning and selling decisions.

  • Weather-based risk assessment and field activity guidance
  • Market price analysis to support sell / hold decisions
  • Transparent, explainable advisories with clear recommendations
